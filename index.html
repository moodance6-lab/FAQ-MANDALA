What is MandalaChain?

Mandala Chain is a hybrid Layer 1 blockchain built on the Polkadot network.

It aims to bridge the gap between emerging and global blockchain markets by integrating government, enterprise, and retail applications on a decentralized platform.

Its primary focus is on Indonesia as its initial use case, facilitating blockchain adoption through secure digital identity infrastructure and scalable solutions across various industries.

Mandala Chain provides a sovereign chain framework built on Substrate that allows governments and enterprises to quickly launch real-world blockchain solutions. It emphasizes secure and confidential data storage and transfer, addressing concerns about data breaches in today's digital environment.

What is IDCHAIN??
IDCHAIN on Mandala Chain is a revolutionary digital identity solution built on top of the Mandala blockchain platform, designed to develop a secure digital identity infrastructure using Indonesia's country code top-level domain, .id.

It enables users to safely store identity documents and interact seamlessly with government and enterprise portals.

Key features include:

1. Collaboration with Indonesia's official manager of the .id domain.

2. Secure and decentralized identity storage.

3. Empowering users to authenticate and verify identity online with enhanced privacy and control.

4. Supporting the goal to onboard over 10 million+ users in Indonesia to blockchain-based digital identity services.

This makes IDCHAIN a significant step forward in creating trusted Web3 and traditional digital identity systems for Indonesia, ensuring safer access to government and business services online.

web what is parachain?

A parachain is a type of blockchain that runs parallel to a main blockchain, usually within a larger ecosystem like Polkadot. It allows multiple blockchains to operate together, sharing security and interoperability while maintaining their own unique features.

Think of parachains as individual lanes on a highway that connect to the main road, enabling smooth and fast communication between different blockchains.

Parachains benefit from the security of the main relay chain (the main blockchain), but they can have their own rules and tokens. This setup makes it easier for different blockchain projects to work together seamlessly.

If you're curious to dive deeper, you can look into how Polkadot uses parachains to create a decentralized multi-chain network!
